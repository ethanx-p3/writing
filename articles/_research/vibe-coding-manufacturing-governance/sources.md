# Annotated Bibliography: Where Vibe Coding Breaks Down

**Article ID:** `vibe-coding-manufacturing-governance`

---

1. **KPMG / TxMinds — Low-Code Governance Framework**  
   txminds.com/blog/low-code-governance-citizen-development  
   Key stat: 73% of low-code planners have no governance rules. Use to open the governance gap argument.

2. **Veracode 2025 GenAI Code Security Report** (via Baytech Consulting)  
   baytechconsulting.com/blog/ai-vibe-coding-security-risk-2025  
   Key stat: 45% of AI-generated code has security flaws. Primary data for vibe coding risk argument.

3. **Microsoft Power Platform CoE — governance penalty data**  
   valoremreply.com/resources/insights/blog/power-platform-at-scale  
   Key stat: 3-4x more sprawl/violations without governance. Quantifies the cost of the gap.

4. **ISA.org — ISA-95 Standard**  
   isa.org/standards-and-publications/isa-standards/isa-95-standard  
   Primary source for ISA-95 level definitions. Cite directly.

5. **IT/OT Insider — ISA-95 and Purdue Model Explained**  
   itotinsider.substack.com/p/isa-95-and-the-purdue-model-explained  
   Best accessible explainer of the 5-level hierarchy. Good for readers unfamiliar with ISA-95.

6. **G2 / Productiv — Shadow IT Statistics**  
   track.g2.com/resources/shadow-it-statistics  
   Key stats: 42% of apps are shadow IT; 38% of employees driven there by slow IT response.

7. **Protiviti — Manufacturing OT Security Challenges (2026)**  
   blog.protiviti.com/2026/01/16/manufacturings-ot-security-challenges  
   Key stat: ransomware against industrial operators up 46% in one quarter. Stakes of OT exposure.

8. **Tulip — Build, Govern, Scale event recap**  
   tulip.co/blog/build-govern-scale-key-takeaways-from-the-virtual-event  
   Primary source for Tulip's governance model. Best real-world example of paved-road approach.

9. **AI Laws Blog — EU Cyber Resilience Act and vibe coding**  
   blog.ai-laws.org/bad-vibes-for-vipe-coding  
   Regulatory framing. CRA compliance risk for manufacturing software. Good for VP IT audience.

10. **Hakuna Matata Tech — Hidden Factory concept**  
    hakunamatatatech.com/our-resources/blog/hidden-factory  
    Historical grounding: shadow operations in manufacturing predate software. 20-40% capacity stat.

11. **Microsoft Learn — Power Platform Governance 2025 Wave 1**  
    learn.microsoft.com/en-us/power-platform/release-plan/2025wave1  
    Official governance roadmap. Shows enterprise platforms are investing in this — not just a niche concern.

12. **Siemens — ISA-95 Framework and Layers**  
    siemens.com/en-us/technology/isa-95-framework-layers  
    Siemens' framing of ISA-95 — relevant given their ownership of Mendix and position in manufacturing software.
